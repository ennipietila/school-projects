The player is standing in the lobby of a mall.
In order to win this game, you first have to go up to the second floor and get your wallet.
Then you come back down and enter the bank (north), where you have to withdraw some cash so you can buy the gift.
When you have the cash, go back to the lobby and from there, enter the gift shop (east). 
Here you can buy the gift. All you need to do now is find your way to the parking lot, and the game is completed.
